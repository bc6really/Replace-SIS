LOI.2 Amendments to Specifications

Any amendments to or errors in the specifications brought to the attention of the Institute will be added to or corrected and distributed to all those holding specifications.

LOI.3 Letter of Intent-to-Respond

Proposers wishing to participate in this RFP process must complete the form in section LOI.5 to indicate an Intent-to-Respond. Forms must be received by the date provided in the schedule found in section LOI.1 of this RFP. The letter must state the intent of the proposer to participate in the process and their acceptance of the RFP evaluation criteria, process, and the instructions of the RFP. The letter should also include the name, email, complete address and phone number of a designated individual to whom any correspondence/addendums should be sent.

LOI.4 Communications

In order to most effectively and efficiently communicate with proposers, the Institute is asking all vendors that have an interest in possibly submitting a response to this RFP, to provide this form to the Institute. The form is in section LOI.5. The purpose of this “Letter of Intent” is to identify contact personnel who should receive all bid amendments and other possible bid correspondence throughout the course of this solicitation. E-mail will be used to communicate with all those who provide a “letter of intent”. Proposers should recognize that failing to provide a “Letter of Intent” will result in reduced time to consider and respond to RFP correspondence and documentation.

Your “letter of intent” should be submitted by [date two] to ensure immediate consideration; however, the Institute will accept “Letter of Intent to Respond” after this date.
